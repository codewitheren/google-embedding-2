Time management is an essential skill that helps people use their time efficiently and achieve their goals more effectively. In today’s fast-paced world, individuals often have many responsibilities such as work, education, and personal life, which makes proper planning very important. By organizing tasks, setting priorities, and avoiding distractions, people can complete their work more productively and reduce stress. Good time management also allows individuals to create balance in their lives, giving them time for rest, hobbies, and social activities. As a result, those who manage their time well are usually more successful, focused, and satisfied with their daily lives.
