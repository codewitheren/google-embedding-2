Technology has transformed the way people learn and access information in modern education. With the help of the internet, online courses, digital libraries, and educational applications, students can learn almost anything from anywhere in the world. Teachers can also use multimedia tools, interactive platforms, and virtual classrooms to make lessons more engaging and effective. This digital transformation allows students to learn at their own pace and explore subjects in greater depth. Although technology cannot completely replace traditional teaching methods, it has become an important support system that improves learning opportunities and makes education more accessible for many people.
